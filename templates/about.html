About David Simulator 2018



The David Simulator is an authentic simulation of life,
based on the real person named David.

David is a university student living in a shared flat in east Berlin.
His unique character is optimal to portray the joys and challenges of everyday life in the year 2018 and his story will thus serve as a time capsule for future generations.

The idea and creation of the David Simulator eminated from his flatmate Leon who felt inspired and fascinated by Davids presence.
With the help of modern technology and the support of numerous friends the remarkable David-Experience can now be recognized by everyone...


The David Simulator will soon be available in English.
